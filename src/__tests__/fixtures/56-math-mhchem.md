# Chemistry (mhchem) formulas

Basic molecules and ions: water $\ce{H2O}$, carbon dioxide $\ce{CO2}$, sulfate $\ce{SO4^{2-}}$, ammonium $\ce{NH4+}$, calcium hydroxide $\ce{Ca(OH)2}$, hydrated copper $\ce{[Cu(H2O)4]^2+}$.

Combustion with a CJK reaction condition:

$$
\ce{2H2 + O2 ->[点燃] 2H2O}
$$

Decomposition with Delta and a gas arrow:

$$
\ce{2KMnO4 ->[\Delta] K2MnO4 + MnO2 + O2 ^}
$$

Precipitation:

$$
\ce{Ag+ + Cl- -> AgCl v}
$$

$$
\ce{CO2 + Ca(OH)2 -> CaCO3 v + H2O}
$$

Reversible industrial synthesis with stacked conditions:

$$
\ce{N2 + 3H2 <=>[\text{催化剂}][\text{高温、高压}] 2NH3}
$$

Organic: methane $\ce{CH4}$, ethanol $\ce{CH3CH2OH}$, ethylene $\ce{CH2=CH2}$, acetylene $\ce{HC\equiv CH}$, benzene $\ce{C6H6}$ or $\ce{C6H5-}$, glucose $\ce{C6H12O6}$.

Electrochemistry:

$$
\ce{Zn - 2e- -> Zn^2+}
$$

$$
\ce{MnO4- + 8H+ + 5e- -> Mn^2+ + 4H2O}
$$

Nuclear chemistry with mass and atomic numbers:

$$
\ce{^{14}_{6}C -> ^{14}_{7}N + ^{-1}_{0}e}
$$

$$
\ce{^{235}_{92}U + ^{1}_{0}n -> ^{141}_{56}Ba + ^{92}_{36}Kr + 3^{1}_{0}n}
$$

Physical units: molar enthalpy $\pu{123 kJ/mol}$, concentration $\pu{0.1 mol/L}$.
